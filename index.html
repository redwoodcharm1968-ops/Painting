About Us

Meet The Painting Company

We take immense pride in delivering top-tier painting services to Duluth and every community within a 100-mile radius, from Hermantown and Two Harbors to Cloquet and beyond. Every project receives the same uncompromising attention to detail, rigorous safety standards, and relentless focus on customer satisfaction.

Whether it's a single accent wall or a full exterior restoration, we treat your property with the care and precision it deserves, using only Sherwin-Williams products for finishes that last.
