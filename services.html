Services Built for Perfection

We don't cut corners — because the finish you see is only as good as the prep work you don't. Every project uses premium Sherwin-Williams products for longevity and a flawless result.

Interior Painting

Premium Interior Painting

Your home's interior is your sanctuary. We treat every room with the same level of care — protecting your furniture, repairing imperfections, and delivering a finish so smooth you'll want to touch every wall.

We exclusively use Sherwin-Williams Duration Home and Emerald Interior — premium lines known for rich color, exceptional coverage, and washability that stands up to real life.

  • Complete furniture and flooring protection with premium drop cloths and plastic sheeting
  • Drywall repair, crack filling, and surface sanding for a perfectly smooth canvas
  • Precision cutting-in on trim, ceilings, and architectural details
  • Thorough daily cleanup — your home stays livable throughout the project
Exterior Painting

Weather-Defying Exterior Painting

Duluth weather is no joke. Lake-effect snow, freeze-thaw cycles, and relentless UV exposure demand a painting system built to last — not just look good on day one.

Our exterior process starts with thorough power washing, scraping, and priming — then we apply Sherwin-Williams Duration Exterior and SuperPaint, engineered for maximum adhesion and fade resistance in harsh climates.

  • Professional power washing to remove dirt, mildew, and chalking
  • Scraping, sanding, and caulking of all failing surfaces
  • Premium primer application for superior topcoat adhesion
  • Two-coat premium topcoat system for lasting protection through MN winters
