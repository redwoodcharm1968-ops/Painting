Our Story

Rooted in Duluth

The Painting Company was founded in Duluth with a simple belief: homeowners in this community deserve painting craftsmanship that matches the beauty of Lake Superior itself.

After years of watching shortcuts and subpar materials undermine beautiful homes across the Twin Ports, Gus and Brea set out to build something different — a painting company where quality isn't negotiable, timelines are honored, and every client feels like the only client.

Today, we're proud to serve Duluth, Hermantown, Proctor, Cloquet, Two Harbors, and the surrounding North Shore communities. Every project we take on strengthens the trust our neighbors have placed in us — and that's a responsibility we never take lightly.
